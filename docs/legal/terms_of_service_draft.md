# Questra Terms of Service Draft

## Draft Status

This is a product draft for internal beta planning. It requires human legal
review before any public release or external distribution.

## Service Summary

Questra helps users shape personal Quests, break them into Missions, record
Trails, reflect with Arc, and participate in Guild-oriented progress loops.

## User Responsibilities

- Use Questra for lawful personal planning, reflection, and collaboration.
- Do not upload content that violates another person's rights.
- Do not use Guild features to harass, impersonate, or disclose another
  person's private information.
- Keep account credentials secure.

## User Content

Users may create Quest, Mission, Trail, Reflection, Guild, profile, and media
content. Users retain ownership of their content, while granting Questra the
limited rights needed to store, process, display, and operate the service.

## Arc

Arc is presented as a navigator for the user's journey in Questra. Arc guidance
is part of the product experience and should be reviewed by the user before
acting on it.

## Media Uploads

Users are responsible for images and other media they upload. Private Trail
media is intended to remain private unless future sharing controls are
explicitly added and enabled by the user.

## Guild Conduct

Guild spaces should support constructive Quest and Trail progress. Questra may
remove content or restrict access when Guild conduct creates safety, privacy, or
abuse concerns.

## Account and Data Requests

Before public release, Questra must provide a documented channel for account
deletion, data deletion, and data export requests.

## Disclaimers

Questra is not a medical, legal, financial, or emergency service. Users should
seek qualified professional support for high-stakes decisions.
