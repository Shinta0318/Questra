# QST-105 Visible Scrollbar System

## Status

Done

## Summary

Added an opt-in, always-visible Questra scrollbar for long content surfaces.

## Implementation

- Converted `QuestraResponsiveListView` to own a fallback `ScrollController`.
- Added `showScrollbar` for surfaces where scrollability is not obvious.
- The Scrollbar and ListView share the same controller.
- The scrollbar remains interactive on mouse and trackpad platforms.
- Added a slim Gold scrollbar theme that fits the Questra visual system.
- Enabled visible scrollbars on Quest List, Trail Timeline/List, Guild Feed,
  and Arc Chat history.
- Other short or form-focused surfaces remain free of unnecessary scrollbars.
