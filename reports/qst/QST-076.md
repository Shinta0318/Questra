# QST-076 Report: Mission Reminder / Signal MVP

## Summary

Added an in-app Signal foundation for Mission and Quest progress. Signals are deterministic, gentle, and do not require platform notification permissions. Home now shows the most relevant Signal, and Mission shows up to three actionable Signals.

## Implementation Notes

- Added `MissionSignalService` with deterministic rules for overdue Quest, due-soon Quest, stale Mission, and suggested small step.
- Home displays the highest-priority Signal as a compact card.
- Mission screen displays a Signal panel before the Mission list.
- Copy is gentle and avoids shame language.
- No push notification permission or platform notification integration was introduced.

## Acceptance Check

- Signals can be generated from Mission and Quest context.
- Home shows the most relevant Signal.
- Mission surface shows contextual Signals.
- Signals use gentle Arc/Questra language and do not shame users.
- No platform notification permission is required for MVP.
