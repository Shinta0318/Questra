# QST-101 Responsive Design Audit

## Status

Done

## Summary

Audited Home, Quest, Mission, Trail, Guild, Arc Chat, Profile, and onboarding
for compact, medium, and expanded widths.

## Results

- Confirmed that major screens generally have `SafeArea` and vertical scrolling.
- Identified missing shared breakpoint and maximum content-width rules.
- Identified fixed rows, chat input, bottom sheets, and timeline metadata as the
  main compact-width and text-scaling risks.
- Documented per-screen risks and follow-up ownership in
  `docs/product/responsive_design_audit.md`.
