# QST-053 Report: Arc Concern System

## Summary

Added deterministic Arc concern rules and a Quest-surface concern card. Arc can now notice overdue active Quests, stale open Missions, and low recent Trail activity, then offer one small next step without blame.

## Changed Files

- `apps/mobile/lib/features/arc/arc_concern_service.dart`
- `apps/mobile/lib/features/quest/quest_screen.dart`
- `apps/mobile/test/arc_concern_service_test.dart`
- `docs/qst/BACKLOG.yaml`
- `docs/product/qst_backlog.md`
- `reports/qst/QST-053.md`

## Implementation Notes

- Added `ArcConcernService` with deterministic rules:
  - overdue active Quest with incomplete progress
  - open Mission older than three days
  - active Quest with no Trail in the last seven days
- Added `ArcConcern` model with type, message, action label, target ids, and emotion.
- Quest screen now shows a warm Arc concern card when a concern exists.
- Low activity routes to Trail; Quest/Mission concerns route to the related Quest.

## Acceptance Check

- Arc can express concern without blame or pressure.
- Concern state uses recent inactivity and open work signals.
- Concern guidance offers one small next step.
