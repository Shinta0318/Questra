## Implementation Notes

- Added `ArcBondGrowthService`.
- Bond deltas are deterministic:
  - Quest created: +5
  - Quest updated: +1
  - Mission created: +2
  - Mission completed: +4
  - Trail posted: +3
  - Trail reflection: +5
  - Arc conversation: +1
- Bond is capped at 100.
- `AuthController.addBondScore` updates local profile state and persists `bond_score` when Supabase is configured.
- Growth hooks run only after the core persistence path has succeeded.

## Acceptance Check

- Bond grows from Quest, Mission, Trail, Reflection, and Arc interaction signals.
- Rules are deterministic and testable.
- Growth is capped to avoid runaway increments.
