# QST-090 Feature Expansion Review

## Status

Done

## Review Summary

QST-073 through QST-089 form a coherent feature expansion for the internal
beta. Arc companionship, Quest planning, Trail retention, Guild usefulness,
recommendation foundations, future Arc rendering, onboarding personalization,
and privacy-conscious analytics are connected without introducing a paywall or
replacing the MVP core loop.

No release-blocking regression was identified in this review.

## Stability Evidence

- `flutter analyze`: passed after QST-089.
- `flutter test`: passed after QST-089 (103 tests).
- Quest, Mission, Trail, Guild, and Arc terminology remains consistent.
- Arc is not presented as an AI assistant in user-facing copy.
- Premium readiness remains flag-only and does not restrict MVP behavior.

## Remaining Risks

1. Major screens need compact, medium, and expanded viewport validation.
2. Real-device beta account setup and first-run persistence need verification.
3. Empty database states need testing with clean beta accounts.
4. Crash, Supabase error, and Arc fallback evidence collection needs a beta plan.
5. Privacy, terms, and AI/data explanations require human review before launch.

## Roadmap Decision

The next formal roadmap is QST-101 through QST-130:

- QST-101 through QST-120: responsive UX foundation and cross-device review.
- QST-121 through QST-130: beta account, feedback, legal, release, and go/no-go
  preparation.

QST-101 Responsive Design Audit is the next Ready item.
