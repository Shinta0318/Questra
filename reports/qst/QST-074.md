# QST-074 Report: Arc Action Trigger Rules

## Summary

Added a centralized Arc action trigger service that maps major MVP actions to Arc emotion, timeline source, and short Japanese copy. Quest, Mission, Trail, Arc Chat, and Home now consume the same decision layer instead of scattering hard-coded Arc reactions.

## Implementation Notes

- Added `ArcActionTriggerService` and `ArcActionDecision`.
- Centralized mappings for Quest creation/update, Mission creation/completion, Trail posting, Reflection, Arc Chat response, unauthenticated state, save failure, and inactivity concern.
- Wired decisions into Arc Emotion Timeline recording for Quest, Mission, Trail, and Arc Chat.
- Added a Home `Arc Signal` card that surfaces the latest Arc reaction, or a gentle inactivity signal when no event exists yet.
- Kept Arc wording as a companion/navigator experience and avoided AI assistant framing.

## Acceptance Check

- Trigger rules are centralized and testable.
- Major MVP actions resolve an Arc emotion and short Japanese message.
- Home, Arc Chat, Quest, Mission, and Trail surfaces consume trigger decisions.
- Failure and unauthenticated states use gentle Arc language.
- No visible Story terminology was introduced.
